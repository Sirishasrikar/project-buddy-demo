Portfolio Overview

Big-picture control. The Portfolio View lets you oversee multiple projects at once:

  • A portfolio dashboard lists each project with a quick status indicator (like green for on track, yellow for at risk) and progress percentage.
  • Your personal “My Tasks” inbox shows every task assigned to you (or awaiting your approval) across all projects in one consolidated list.

It’s like a control center for everything you’re working on, so you can juggle multiple projects without losing track of any details.
