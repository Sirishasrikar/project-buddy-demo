Task Approvals & Sign-Offs

Stay compliant with ease. Built-in approval workflows ensure critical tasks get proper sign-off:

  • Tasks needing approval are clearly labeled (with an icon or note) and cannot be marked done until the right person approves.
  • Approvers see a dedicated “Approve” button on those tasks, making sign-off as easy as one click.
  • Every approval action is timestamped and recorded, providing an audit trail of who approved what and when.

This means nothing important is completed without the right person's authorization, giving you confidence in compliance and quality control.
